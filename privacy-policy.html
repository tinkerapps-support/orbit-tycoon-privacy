Privacy Policy

Orbit Tycoon  ·  Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

Orbit Tycoon is a mobile game developed by Tinker Apps. Tinker Apps does not intentionally collect or store personal data on its own servers. However, Tinker Apps configures and integrates certain third-party services (such as Google services) that may process data independently as described in this policy. Tinker Apps does not control how Google or its services collect, use, or share data. Tinker Apps does not sell personal data. All Google services referenced in this policy operate under Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Controller

The data controller responsible for this app is Tinker Apps (United States). You can contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com.

Data We Collect

Tinker Apps does not collect or store personal data on its own servers. Game progress — including credits, upgrades, inventory, and settings — is stored locally on your device. If you sign in to Google Play Games, this progress data is synced to your Google account. Game save data is designed not to include directly identifying personal information.

Google AdMob

Orbit Tycoon displays rewarded ads served by Google AdMob. If you watch a rewarded ad, AdMob may independently collect:

  • Device identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

This data is collected by AdMob and shared with Google and its advertising partners for ad targeting and measurement. Tinker Apps does not control how AdMob collects or uses this data. You can opt out of personalised advertising and limit certain ad-related data collection in your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalisation. AdMob is operated by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Ads in the EEA, UK & Switzerland

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, Orbit Tycoon is configured to request non-personalised ads only. Non-personalised ads are not targeted based on behavioural profiling or personalised advertising profiles. Google may still process limited contextual, security, fraud prevention, or operational data necessary to deliver ads in accordance with applicable law and Google's own policies.

Personalised ad targeting is disabled for users in these regions. You do not need to take any action to opt out of personalised advertising.

Google Play Billing & In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. Tinker Apps does not receive or store any payment information. All purchase validation is handled by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Orbit Tycoon offers the following types of in-app purchases:

  • Token bundles (100 / 400 / 1,000 tokens) — consumable, one-time grants. Tokens cannot be restored in the event of data loss, device change, or account transfer.
  • No Ads Pack — includes a one-time grant of 50 tokens (non-restorable) plus permanent benefits: ad removal and 2× material haul. The permanent benefits are non-consumable and linked to your Google Play account; they restore automatically on reinstall.
  • Starter Pack — includes a one-time grant of 100 tokens (non-restorable) plus 2 permanent bonus fleet slots. The fleet slots are non-consumable and linked to your Google Play account; they restore automatically on reinstall.

Token grants (including those bundled with packs) are not recoverable if game data is deleted or lost. If you uninstall and reinstall the app, only non-consumable benefits will be restored automatically via your Google Play account.

Google Play Games Services

The app optionally integrates with Google Play Games Services for sign-in, cloud saves, and achievement tracking. If you do not sign in to Google Play Games, Play Games profile data is not shared. However, other integrated Google services (such as AdMob and Firebase) may still process data as described elsewhere in this policy.

If you do sign in, Google Play Games Services independently collects the following for app functionality purposes:

  • User ID (Gamer ID) — a unique identifier linking your Google account to your in-game profile
  • App activity — achievement progress and cloud save data used to power Google Play Games features

Tinker Apps does not control how Google collects or uses this data, and does not access, store, or share it. Google Play Games Services operates under Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

Orbit Tycoon uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the game. Firebase Analytics may automatically collect:

  • App events (e.g. session start, first open, in-app purchases)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • A randomly-generated app instance ID
  • Approximate location derived from IP address

This data is processed by Google under Google's Privacy Policy. Tinker Apps uses this data in aggregate only to improve gameplay and does not use it to directly identify individual users. You may be able to limit certain data collection through your device or Google privacy settings, though some analytics or diagnostic data may still be collected to support app functionality.

Firebase Crashlytics

Orbit Tycoon uses Google Firebase Crashlytics to automatically detect and report app crashes and errors. When a crash occurs, Crashlytics may collect:

  • Stack traces and crash logs
  • Device state at the time of the crash (device model, OS version, available memory)
  • A randomly-generated installation UUID

This data is used solely to diagnose and fix bugs and is not used to identify individual users. Crashlytics is operated by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

International Data Transfers

Google services integrated into Orbit Tycoon (AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google Play Games Services, and Google Play Billing) may transfer and process data in the United States and other countries outside the EEA, the UK, and Switzerland. Google relies on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission as the legal mechanism for these transfers. For more information, see Google's data transfer frameworks.

Data Retention

Tinker Apps does not retain personal data on its own servers. Retention periods for data processed by Google services are governed by Google's own policies:

  • AdMob — ad interaction and identifier data is retained per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics — event data is retained for up to 14 months by default; aggregate data may be retained indefinitely.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports are retained for 90 days.
  • Google Play Games Services — cloud save and achievement data is retained until you delete it via your Google account.

Local game data is retained on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data in your device settings.

Data Deletion

Tinker Apps does not store your data on its own infrastructure. To manage or remove data:

  • Local game data — uninstall the app or clear app data in your device settings.
  • Cloud save and achievement data — delete via your Google Play Games profile or Google Account settings under Settings > Delete Play Games account and data.
  • AdMob data — managed by Google; visit myaccount.google.com.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics data — managed by Google; visit myaccount.google.com to manage data associated with your Google account.

Some data collected by third-party services (such as Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics) may not be individually identifiable or deletable, but is used in aggregate and retained according to Google's data retention policies.

Your Rights (EEA, UK & Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the GDPR and equivalent laws. Because Tinker Apps does not hold personal data directly, most of these rights apply to data held by Google and can be exercised at myaccount.google.com. You may also contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com and we will assist where reasonably possible or direct you to the appropriate Google privacy controls.

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data held about you.
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to restriction of processing — request that processing of your data be limited in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format where processing is based on consent or contract.
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In the EU, find your authority at edpb.europa.eu. In the UK, contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Children's Privacy

Orbit Tycoon is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone below the minimum digital consent age in their jurisdiction — age 13 in the United States, or up to age 16 in certain EEA member states. If you believe a child below the applicable age has used the app in a way that has transmitted personal data, please contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

Security

Tinker Apps does not store personal data on its own servers. Local game data is protected by Android's standard application sandboxing. Cloud saves are secured by your Google account credentials. Data transmitted to Google services (AdMob, Firebase, Play Games, Play Billing) is handled under Google's security practices. No method of electronic transmission is completely secure.

Legal Basis for Processing

Tinker Apps does not intentionally collect or store personal data on its own servers, but does determine how certain third-party services are configured within the app. Where we act as a data controller in that capacity, we rely on the following legal bases under GDPR and equivalent laws:

  • Legitimate interests — Firebase Crashlytics is used to maintain app stability and security. Crash data is not used by Tinker Apps to directly identify users by name, email, or similar directly identifying information.
  • Legitimate interests or consent where required by law — Firebase Analytics may be used to understand aggregate gameplay trends. Where applicable law requires a stronger legal basis for analytics involving device identifiers, we rely on that basis or limit collection accordingly.
  • Non-personalised ads (EEA, UK & Switzerland) — users in these regions are served non-personalised ads only. Tinker Apps does not enable personalised ad targeting for users in these regions.

For data processed directly by Google services, Google acts as an independent data controller and relies on its own legal bases as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
