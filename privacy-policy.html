Privacy Policy

Orbit Tycoon  ·  Last updated: April 11, 2026

Overview

Orbit Tycoon is a mobile game developed by Tinker Apps. Tinker Apps does not collect personal data directly. However, the app integrates Google services for advertising, in-app purchases, and optional game features — these services may collect data as described below.

Data We Collect

Tinker Apps does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its own servers. Game progress — including credits, upgrades, inventory, and settings — is stored locally on your device. If you sign in to Google Play Games, this progress data is synced to your Google account. The saved game state contains no personal information intentionally.

Google AdMob

Orbit Tycoon displays rewarded ads served by Google AdMob. If you watch a rewarded ad, AdMob may independently collect:

  • Device identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data

This data is collected by AdMob and shared with Google and its advertising partners for ad targeting and measurement. Tinker Apps does not control how AdMob collects or uses this data. You can opt out of personalised advertising and limit certain ad-related data collection in your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalisation. AdMob is operated by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Billing

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. Tinker Apps does not receive or store any payment information. All purchase validation is handled by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Games Services

The app optionally integrates with Google Play Games Services for sign-in, cloud saves, and achievement tracking. If you do not sign in, no personal data leaves your device.

If you do sign in, Google Play Games Services independently collects the following for app functionality purposes:

  • User ID (Gamer ID) — a unique identifier linking your Google account to your in-game profile
  • App activity — achievement progress and cloud save data used to power Google Play Games features

Tinker Apps does not control how Google collects or uses this data, and does not access, store, or share it. Google Play Games Services operates under Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion

Tinker Apps does not store your data on its own infrastructure. To manage or remove data:

  • Local game data — uninstall the app or clear app data in your device settings.
  • Cloud save and achievement data — delete via your Google Play Games profile or Google Account settings under Settings > Delete Play Games account and data.
  • AdMob data — managed by Google; visit myaccount.google.com.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access or delete personal data. Because Tinker Apps holds no personal data directly, these rights apply to data held by Google. You can exercise them at myaccount.google.com or by contacting us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Orbit Tycoon is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app in a way that has transmitted personal data, please contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

Security

Tinker Apps does not store personal data on its own servers. Local game data is protected by Android's standard application sandboxing. Cloud saves are secured by your Google account credentials. Data transmitted to Google services (AdMob, Play Games, Play Billing) is handled under Google's security practices. No method of electronic transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
