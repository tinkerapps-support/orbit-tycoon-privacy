Privacy Policy

Orbit Tycoon  ·  Last updated: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Orbit Tycoon ("the App") handles information when you play the game on Android devices.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). The App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use its core features. However, third-party services integrated into the App (such as advertising providers) may collect personal data as described in Section 2.

Game progress data — including credits, upgrades, inventory, research, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. If you sign in to Google Play Games, this game data may also be saved to your Google account via the Google Play Games cloud save feature. No personal data is included in the saved game state.

2. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services. Each service operates under its own privacy policy and may collect data independently of the App. Third-party services may collect technical information such as device type, operating system, advertising identifiers, IP address, and approximate location.

a) Google AdMob

The App displays rewarded advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
https://policies.google.com/privacy
https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

b) Google Play Billing

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play. The App does not receive or store your payment information. Purchase validation is handled entirely by Google Play.
https://policies.google.com/privacy

c) Google Play Games Services

Sign-in and cloud saves are optional and provided by Google Play Games Services. If you sign in, your Google account information is processed by Google under their privacy policy. The App only stores game progress data (no PII) in the cloud save.
https://policies.google.com/privacy

d) International Data Transfers

Third-party services integrated into the App may process data on servers located outside your country. By using the App, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred internationally in accordance with the third-party services' own privacy policies.

3. How We Use Information

Game progress data is used solely to save and restore your game state across sessions and devices. We do not sell your personal data. We do not share personal data directly; however, third-party services integrated into the App may collect and process data independently as described in Section 2.

Where applicable, we process data on the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interest — to support core app functionality.
  • Consent — for personalised advertising served by AdMob, where required by applicable law.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict its processing. Because the App itself does not collect personal data, most of these rights apply to data held by third-party services (Google). You can exercise those rights directly through your Google account at myaccount.google.com.

For any privacy-related requests, you may also contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Local game data is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing App data in your device settings.

Cloud save data (if you used Google Play Games sign-in) can be deleted through your Google account at myaccount.google.com or via the Google Play Games app under Settings > Delete Play Games account and data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not designed for or directed at children under the age of 13, and does not knowingly target them with advertising. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Local game data is protected by Android's standard application sandboxing, and cloud saves are protected by your Google account credentials. We do not operate any servers that store your data. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at the URL linked in the App's Play Store listing. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
