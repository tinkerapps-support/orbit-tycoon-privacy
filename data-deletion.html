Data Deletion

Orbit Tycoon  ·  Tinker Apps

Tinker Apps does not store your personal data on its own servers. Data associated with Orbit Tycoon is held either locally on your device or by Google services. Follow the steps below to delete or manage each type of data.

Local Game Data

Your game progress (credits, upgrades, inventory, settings) is stored locally on your device.

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Apps (or Application Manager)
  3. Find and tap Orbit Tycoon
  4. Tap Storage > Clear Data

Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all local data.

Cloud Save & Achievement Data (Google Play Games)

If you signed in to Google Play Games, your save data and achievements are stored in your Google account.

  1. Open the Google Play Games app
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top right
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Tap Delete Play Games account and data
  5. Follow the on-screen steps to confirm

You can also manage this at myaccount.google.com under Data & Privacy.

Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

AdMob uses your Android Advertising ID for ad personalisation. You can reset or limit this:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Google > Ads
  3. Tap Reset advertising ID to unlink past ad data
  4. Or tap Opt out of Ads Personalisation to limit future data use

For broader AdMob data managed by Google, visit myaccount.google.com.

Analytics & Crash Data (Firebase)

Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics collect anonymous usage and crash data. This data is not tied to your name or account and cannot be individually deleted. However, you can limit future collection:

  1. Clear app data (see Local Game Data above) — this resets your Firebase app instance ID, unlinking future data from past sessions
  2. To limit ad-related signals, follow the AdMob opt-out steps above
Some analytics and crash data may be retained in aggregate by Google according to their data retention policies. This data is not individually identifiable and cannot be specifically requested for deletion.
